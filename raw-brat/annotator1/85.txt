  Outcomes:

The primary objective of this study was to investigate the efficacy of methylprednisolone pulse on mortality rate, blood O2 saturation, and need for further oxygen therapy. Sequential Organ Failure Assessment (SOFA) Score changes were assessed before and during the study. Moreover radiographic and laboratory parameters including Creatine phosphokinase (CPK), lactate dehydrogenase (LDH), and serum creatinine (SrCr), Total and Direct bilirubin were studied.
